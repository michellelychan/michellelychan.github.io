The Boola Website Design
Designing Yale's Social Media and News Platform

THE TASK

I was approached by the President of The Boola at Yale to work on the redesign of the website. I researched on similar social news aggregator sites and made design prototypes that matches with The Boola's needs and the audience.

ABOUT THE BOOLA

The Boola is a social news site for students at Yale, featuring articles that relate to life in New Haven ranging from best and worst food around campus, the things you'll complain about from class, lifestyle choices and thought processes, and more. The website:theboola.comt.
