THE TASK

I managed the end-to-end design to development process of the Kara Smart Fitness smart mirror and mobile app. The smart mirror provides users with workout classes uses computer vision to assess the users' performance and provides workout suggestions.

ABOUT Kara Smart Fitness

Kara Smart Fitness is a product of OliveX, a subsidiary of Animoca Brands that owns The Sandbox, the largest competitor of Roblox, and the worlds top three metaverse tokens ($SAND) by market cap.

The Product

Kara Smart Fitness mirror is the first smart fitness mirror in Hong Kong and Australia. It uses AI and computer vision to track your key body moments and provides you with relevant course recommendations based on your performance.

While our smart fitness mirror is similar to those of Mirror.com, Tempo, and other competitors and open to male and female users, the most prominent user persona in our group is female aged 25 - 55 who may often skip the gym due to family, Covidy, busy schedules, and other reasons. We also provide a mobile app with our workout courses and is featured several times on Apple App Store.

Mirrors offer an interesting their interface to your ambient environment. It is fun to play with different types of human interactions with large screens and computer vision.

From attracting mall passersby to creating AI interactive fitness games

One of our major sales channel other than online marketing is through offline stores and partnerships with malls, hotels, corporate gyms, and more. However, users find it hard to spot anything special about the mirror when they are passing by. Therefore, we introduced a catchy screensaver that brings users to a 3 min AI fitness workout challenge that measures the users performance, compares their performance with other users, and provides them with workout recommendations. Through just 3 mins, users can experience the benefit of the mirror.

We realized that this was a very popular game, and the demo also attracted more sales partnerships. We decided to incorporate a similar type of content into our core consumer mirror product, where users can play an AI interactive fitness game that provides them with workout recommendations. One of the user problems is that users are not sure about their fitness level and the courses that fit. With the AI interactive game, we can measure their performance through cardio and strength exercises and provide them with workout recommenations based on their strength, balance, and cardiovascular fitness.
