Sublite
Website Design
Redesigning the Student Internship & Housing Search Experience

THE TASK

I joined the Sublite team at Yale as a web designer in October 2014. This past year, I designed the marketing page, the internship platform of the website as well as the housing platform. I am currently revamping the internship platform of the website, such as the application process for students and the applicant search platform for recruiters.

ABOUT SUBLITE

Backed by the Yale Venture Creation Program and the Wharton Innovation Fund, SubLite is an exclusive student-to-student online platform to find safe housing for internships. SubLite launched its Beta housing platform this summer, which attracted over 8,500 users from 500 universities. The majority of our current users are students from Yale, Harvard, UPenn, Princeton, Stanford, Columbia and NYU. It is now on www.sublite.net.
